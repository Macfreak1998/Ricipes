## Chapter 3: Gin - From Medicine to Martini

**5 Essential Gin Cocktails** (brief descriptions, not full recipes)

1. **Martini** - The king of gin cocktails, showcases botanical complexity
2. **Negroni** - Equal parts perfection, gin's bitterness meets Campari
3. **Gin & Tonic** - Simple but perfect, quinine and botanicals in harmony

## Selection Philosophy

These examples serve to:
1. **Demonstrate Use Cases**: Show each spirit in its best context
2. **Historical Context**: Connect spirits to classic cocktails
3. **Flavor Profiles**: Illustrate how spirit character shines through
4. **Versatility**: Show range of applications for each spirit
5. **Modern Relevance**: Include contemporary drinks alongside classics
