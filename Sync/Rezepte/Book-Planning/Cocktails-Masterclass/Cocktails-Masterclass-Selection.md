## Selection Philosophy

These 125 cocktails are organized to teach:
1. **Pattern Recognition**: See the template, understand variations
2. **Technique Mastery**: Each family teaches specific skills
3. **Historical Evolution**: How families developed over time
4. **Creative Application**: Learn to create your own variations
5. **Professional Standards**: Bar-quality execution

---

## By Technique

- **Shaken**: 45 drinks (Sours, Egg drinks, Modern classics)
- **Stirred**: 35 drinks (Martinis, Manhattans, Negronis, Old Fashioneds)
- **Built**: 30 drinks (Highballs, Juleps, Tiki)
- **Blended**: 5 drinks (Frozen variations)
- **Muddled**: 10 drinks (Juleps, Smashes, Mojito)

## Learning Progression

**Beginner Families** (Start here):
- Highballs (easy building)
- Collins/Fizz (intro to shaking + soda)
- Simple Sours (basic shaking)

**Intermediate Families** (Build skills):
- Martinis (stirring technique)
- Manhattans (stirred complexity)
- Old Fashioneds (spirit-forward balance)

**Advanced Families** (Master craft):
- Negronis (bitter balance)
- Tiki (multiple spirits)
- Modern Classics (contemporary techniques)

**Expert Families** (Challenge yourself):
- Flips/Nogs (whole egg technique)
- Punches (batch scaling)
- Complex variations

---

*Master these families, and you've mastered cocktails. Everything else is variation.*
