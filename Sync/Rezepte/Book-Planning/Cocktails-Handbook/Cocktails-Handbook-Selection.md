## Selection Criteria

These 150 drinks were chosen based on:
1. **Historical Significance**: Classics that shaped cocktail culture
2. **Popularity**: Drinks people actually order
3. **Teaching Value**: Each demonstrates important techniques
4. **Variety**: Coverage of all major spirits and styles
5. **Modern Relevance**: Including 2024-2025 trending drinks
6. **Skill Progression**: Easy → Intermediate → Advanced in each chapter
7. **No Duplicates**: Each drink appears only once in its primary category

---

## Difficulty Distribution

- **Easy (39 drinks)**: Simple builds, 2-4 ingredients, basic techniques
- **Intermediate (73 drinks)**: Standard shaking/stirring, 4-6 ingredients
- **Advanced (31 drinks)**: Complex techniques, multiple steps, challenging
