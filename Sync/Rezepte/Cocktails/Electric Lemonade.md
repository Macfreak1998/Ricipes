# Zutaten

- 1.5 [[Vodka]]
- 1 [[Blue Curaçao]]
- 4 [[Sync/Rezepte/Cocktails/Lemonade]]
- [[Lemon-Lime Soda]] (splash for topping)

# Zubereitung
[[Shake]]: Vodka, Blue Curaçao, Lemonade with ice until well-chilled
[[Strain]]
Top with lemon-lime soda

# Served
* Glass: [[Sync/Rezepte/Gläser/Highball]]
* Ice: [[Cubed Ice]]
* Garnish: Lemon wheel, lemon twist

# History
The Electric Lemonade is a vibrant, eye-catching cocktail that became a viral sensation on TikTok in 2024-2025, racking up over 2 million views for its bold, electric blue color and refreshing taste. While the exact origins of the recipe are unclear, it emerged from the same American bar culture that gave us other colorful party drinks in the 1980s-90s.

The cocktail represents the intersection of visual appeal and accessibility that defines modern social media-driven drink trends. In a 2024 consumer survey, 35.9% of respondents said visual appeal was the deciding factor in trying a viral drink—twice as influential as health claims. The Electric Lemonade's neon blue hue makes it instantly recognizable and highly shareable on Instagram and TikTok.

**The Blue Curaçao Factor:**

Blue Curaçao, the liqueur responsible for the drink's signature color, is essentially orange curaçao with blue food coloring added. Made from the dried peel of the laraha citrus fruit (a bitter orange native to Curaçao), the liqueur contributes a subtle orange flavor alongside its dramatic color. The combination of orange notes from the curaçao with the tartness of lemon creates a complex citrus profile.

**Visual Appeal:**

The electric blue color is striking against clear ice and becomes even more photogenic when garnished with bright yellow lemon wheels. This contrast makes the drink a favorite for summer parties, poolside gatherings, and beach bars. The addition of lemon-lime soda at the end creates a gentle effervescence and adds sparkle to the presentation.

**Cultural Context:**

The Electric Lemonade belongs to a family of "electric" cocktails that emerged in the 1980s-90s, including the Electric Iced Tea and various blue-hued party drinks. These cocktails prioritized fun, color, and approachability over craft cocktail snobbery. While traditional bartenders might dismiss them as overly sweet or gimmicky, they serve an important role: making cocktails accessible and fun for people who might be intimidated by classic cocktails.

**2024-2025 Viral Resurgence:**

TikTok's algorithm favored the Electric Lemonade's visual drama, with videos showing the transformation from clear vodka and lemonade to brilliant blue when the curaçao is added. The shake-and-reveal format proved irresistible to content creators. The drink became particularly popular among younger drinkers (21-35) who discovered it through Instagram Reels and TikTok rather than traditional bar menus.

**Modern Variations:**

Some bartenders have elevated the Electric Lemonade by using fresh-squeezed lemonade instead of bottled, premium vodka, and high-quality curaçao. Others add fresh mint or basil for an herbaceous note, or substitute the lemon-lime soda with champagne for a more sophisticated version.

The Electric Lemonade proves that in the social media age, a drink's appearance can be just as important as its taste. It represents cocktail culture's playful side and the power of visual storytelling in modern drinking culture.
