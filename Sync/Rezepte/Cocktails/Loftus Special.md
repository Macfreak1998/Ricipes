# Zutaten
- juice of 1 1/2 [[Lime]]
- dashes [[Grenadine]]
- 1/4 [[Simple Syrup]]
- 3/4 [[Cherry Heering]]
- 3/4 [[Apricot Liqueur]]
- 3/4 [[White Rum]]
- 1 1/2 [[Dark Rum]]
- 1 1/2 [[Overproof Rum]]

# History
The "Loftus Special" is a classic cocktail with a somewhat mysterious origin, believed to have emerged in the early 20th century.

The exact beginnings of the Loftus Special are debated. Some theories suggest it was created at a bar called Loftus in New Orleans, while others point to a bartender named Mr. Loftus in London as its inventor. Regardless of its precise origin, the cocktail has maintained its popularity over time.

The name "Loftus Special" likely refers to either the establishment or the individual credited with its creation, though a deeper symbolic meaning is not widely documented.

The Loftus Special is characterized as a refreshing drink that blends fruity flavors with a hint of mint, making it a suitable choice for various occasions, particularly warm evenings. This particular recipe features Lime Juice, Grenadine, Simple Syrup, Cherry Heering, Apricot Liqueur, White Rum, Dark Rum, and Overproof Rum. This complex blend of rums, fruit liqueurs, and citrus creates a potent and flavorful tropical drink, perfect for those who appreciate a rich and refreshing cocktail.
