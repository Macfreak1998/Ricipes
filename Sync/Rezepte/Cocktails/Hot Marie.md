# Zutaten
- 3/4 [[Brandy]]
- 3/4 [[Dark Rum]]
- 1/4 [[Coffee Liqueur]]
- 1 cup hot [[Espresso]]
- [[Simple Syrup]]

# Zubereitung
Heat liquors in heat-resistant glass (do not boil)
Add sugar
Fill with hot coffee
Stir
Top with cream

# Served
* Glass: heat-resistant Glass
* Ice:
* Garnish: lightly whipped cream
