# Zutaten

- 0.75 [[Gin]]
- 0.75 [[Cointreau]]
- 0.75 [[Elderflower Liqueur]]
- 0.75 [[Lemon Juice]]
- [[Absinthe]] rinse

# Zubereitung
Rinse glass with [[Absinthe]], discard excess
[[Shake]]: Gin, Cointreau, Elderflower Liqueur, Lemon Juice with ice
[[Strain]]
