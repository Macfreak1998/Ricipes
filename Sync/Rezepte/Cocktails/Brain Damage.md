# History
The Brain Damage is a layered shot that gets its name from its unusual and slightly unsettling appearance. When made correctly, the ingredients curdle and separate in a way that resembles a brain hemorrhage.

The shot is typically made with peach schnapps, Baileys Irish Cream, and a few drops of grenadine. The grenadine sinks to the bottom and then rises through the other ingredients, creating the signature "bloody" effect. It's a novelty drink that became popular in the 1980s and 1990s, known more for its shocking name and appearance than for its taste.
