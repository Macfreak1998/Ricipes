# Zutaten

- 2 [[Tequila Blanco]]
- 1.5 [[Cranberry Juice]] (100%)
- 0.75 [[Orange Liqueur]]
- 0.75 [[Lime Juice]]
- 0.5 [[Hot Honey Syrup]]

# Zubereitung
[[Shake]]: Tequila, Cranberry Juice, Orange Liqueur, Lime Juice, Hot Honey Syrup with ice until well-chilled
[[Strain]]

# Served
* Glass: [[Margarita Glass]] or [[Coupe]]
* Ice: [[Straight]] or [[Cubed Ice]]
* Garnish: Sugared cranberries
* Rim: Salt (optional)

# History
The Holiday Hot Honey Margarita is a modern festive margarita variation created by New York-based Instagram bartender @drinkswithdanica (Danica). The drink was specifically designed for the holiday season, with the creator noting: "Send this to someone who loves a margarita... Comment GLASS and I'll dm you the link to everything I used here. Hot Honey Margaritas are here for the holidays! I love this festive version with cranberry juice, and I have a feeling you will too."

**The Hot Honey Trend:**

Hot honey (honey infused with chili peppers) became one of the biggest food trends of the 2020s, starting in pizza restaurants and eventually spreading to cocktails. The combination of honey's sweetness with chili heat creates a complex sweet-spicy flavor profile that works beautifully in cocktails.

**Holiday Adaptation:**

This margarita specifically targets the Thanksgiving and Christmas seasons by incorporating:
- **Cranberry Juice**: The quintessential holiday fruit, associated with Thanksgiving dinner
- **Hot Honey Syrup**: Provides warming spice perfect for cold weather
- **Sugared Cranberries**: Creates elegant holiday presentation

**Flavor Profile:**

The drink balances several dimensions:
- **Tart**: From cranberry juice and lime
- **Sweet**: From hot honey syrup and orange liqueur
- **Spicy**: From the chili peppers in hot honey
- **Citrus**: From tequila's agave notes and lime
