# Zutaten
- 3/4 [[Lemon Juice]]
- 3/4 [[Mango Syrup]]
- 1 1/2 [[Pineapple Juice]]
- 1 1/2 [[Dark Rum]]
- 3/4 [[Brandy]]
- [[Fresh Mint]] chunk
- [[Cherry]]

# Zubereitung
[[Shake]]: Lemon Juice, Mango Syrup, Pineapple Juice, Dark Rum, Brandy
Pour into large Highball Glass
Add pineapple chunk and cherry

# Served
* Glass: [[Highball Glass]]
* Ice: [[Crushed Ice]]
* Garnish: [[Pineapple]] chunk, [[Cherry]]
