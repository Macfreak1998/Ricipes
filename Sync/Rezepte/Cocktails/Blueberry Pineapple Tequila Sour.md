# Zutaten

- 2 [[Tequila Blanco]]
- 1.5 [[Pineapple Juice]]
- 0.75 [[Lemon Juice]]
- 0.5 [[Blueberry Syrup]]
- 1 [[Egg White]] (optional)

# Zubereitung
[[Dry Shake]]: All ingredients without ice (if using egg white)
Add ice and [[Shake]]: Until well-chilled
[[Double Strain]]

# Served
* Glass: [[Coupe]]
* Ice: [[Straight]]
* Garnish: Aromatic bitters, dried lime wheel, blueberry

# History
The Blueberry Pineapple Tequila Sour is a modern craft cocktail created by Instagram bartender @beautifulbooze for spring 2024, designed to capture the vibrant, fresh flavors of the season. The cocktail represents contemporary mixology's approach to creating seasonally appropriate drinks by combining tropical and berry elements.

**Spring Cocktail Trends:**

This cocktail embodies several key trends in spring cocktail menus:
- **Berry flavors**: Blueberries signal the transition from citrus-heavy winter drinks to berry-forward spring cocktails
- **Tropical elements**: Pineapple juice adds brightness and tropical appeal as warmer weather approaches
- **Tequila sours**: The rise of tequila as a sophisticated cocktail base beyond margaritas

**Flavor Profile:**

The combination of blueberry and pineapple creates an interesting interplay:
- **Blueberry syrup**: Provides deep berry flavor and natural purple-pink color, plus sweetness
- **Pineapple juice**: Adds tropical brightness, acidity, and helps balance the berry sweetness
- **Lemon juice**: Enhances tartness and adds citrus complexity
- **Tequila blanco**: Provides earthy agave notes that ground the fruit flavors

**The Reverse Dry Shake:**

The recipe notes a specific technique: "Shake WITHOUT ice then add ice & shake again Double strain into glass." This reverse dry shake method (shake first without ice, then with ice) helps create maximum foam from the egg white while still properly chilling and diluting the drink.

**Visual Appeal:**

The finished cocktail likely shows a beautiful gradient from deep purple-pink (from the blueberry syrup) to white foam (from the egg white), with aromatic bitters creating patterns on the foam surface. The dried lime wheel and fresh blueberry garnish add color contrast and Instagram appeal.

**The Gearing Up Message:**

The bartender's note—"Gearing up for Spring! Blueberry Tequila Sour"—positions this as a transitional cocktail, perfect for late winter/early spring when you're craving fresh fruit flavors but aren't quite ready for full summer drinks.

This cocktail demonstrates how modern bartenders create seasonal menus by thoughtfully combining ingredients that signal the time of year, using color, flavor, and presentation to tell a story about the season.
