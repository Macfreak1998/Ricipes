# Zutaten

-   1.5 oz [[Sloe Gin]]
-   1.5 oz [[French Vermouth]]
-   2 dashes [[Orange Bitters]]
-   1 Dash [[Pernod]]

# Zubereitung
[[Stir]]: Sloe Gin, French Vermouth, Orange Bitters, Anisette
[[Strain]] into chilled glass

# Served
*   Glass: [[Cocktail Glass]]
*   Ice: [[Straight]]
*   Garnish: [[Lemon Twist]]

# Kategorien
