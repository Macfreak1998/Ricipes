# Zutaten

- 1 part [[Malibu]] (coconut rum)
- 1 part [[Tequila Blanco]]
- 4 fresh blackberries
- 0.75 part [[Cream of Coconut]]
- 1 part [[Lime Juice]]
- 0.5 part [[Demerara Syrup]]
- Salt for rim

# Zubereitung
[[Muddle]]: Blackberries in shaker
Add: Malibu, Tequila, Cream of Coconut, Lime Juice, Demerara Syrup, ice
[[Shake]]: Until well-chilled
[[Strain]]

# Served
* Glass: [[Margarita Glass]] or [[Rocks Glass]]
* Ice: [[Cubed Ice]]
* Garnish: Blackberries, lime wheel
* Rim: Salt

# History
The Coconut & Blackberry Margarita is a modern tropical variation created by Cardiff-based Instagram bartender @my_cocktail_bible. The drink was described as "Sweet, tangy and a little tropical – meet the Coconut & Blackberry Marg," representing the contemporary trend of creating fruity, Instagram-friendly margarita variations.

**The Margarita Template:**

The classic Margarita (tequila, orange liqueur, lime juice) dates back to the 1930s-40s and has become one of the world's most popular cocktails. Its simple formula has spawned countless variations, from the strawberry margarita to the jalapeño margarita, proving the template's versatility.

**Tropical Transformation:**

This version makes several key modifications:

1. **Split Spirit Base**: Uses both tequila and Malibu (coconut rum), reducing tequila's intensity while adding coconut flavor
2. **Cream of Coconut**: Adds rich coconut sweetness and creamy texture (unlike Malibu's lighter coconut flavor)
3. **Fresh Blackberries**: Muddled for natural berry flavor and color
4. **Demerara Syrup**: Provides deeper, molasses-like sweetness than simple syrup
5. **No Orange Liqueur**: Omitted in favor of coconut elements

**Flavor Profile:**

The combination creates a drink that's:
- **Sweet**: From coconut cream, Malibu, and demerara syrup
- **Tangy**: From lime juice and blackberry tartness
- **Tropical**: From dual coconut elements (Malibu and cream of coconut)
- **Fruity**: From muddled blackberries
- **Creamy**: From cream of coconut

**For Winter Drinking:**

The description notes this is "A fruity twist on a classic margarita - perfect for winter sipping with summer energy." This positioning is clever—the frozen margarita season may be over, but the tropical flavors provide escapism during cold weather. The blackberries (a late summer/early autumn fruit) and coconut's rich, warming quality make this work for cooler months.

**Modern Instagram Aesthetics:**

The deep purple-pink color from muddled blackberries against the white coconut cream creates a visually striking gradient, perfect for social media. The contrast between the black-purple drink and white salt rim enhances visual appeal.

The Coconut & Blackberry Margarita represents how classic cocktails evolve to meet contemporary tastes: sweeter, fruitier, more visually dramatic, and designed for sharing on social media as much as for drinking.
