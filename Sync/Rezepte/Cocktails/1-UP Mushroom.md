# 1-UP Mushroom

A modern tiki cocktail from the "How to Drink" YouTube channel.

## Ingredients
- 1.5 oz (45ml) Plantation OFTD Rum
- 0.75 oz (22.5ml) Overproof Mezcal
- 0.5 oz (15ml) Giffard Banane du Br√©sil
- 0.5 oz (15ml) Coco Lopez
- 0.5 oz (15ml) Pineapple Juice
- 0.5 oz (15ml) Lime Juice
- 2 dashes Peychaud's Bitters

## Instructions
1. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker with ice.
2. Shake until well-chilled.
3. Strain into a tiki mug or a highball glass filled with crushed ice.
4. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and a cherry.

# History
The "1-UP Mushroom" is a modern tiki cocktail, drawing its name and inspiration from the iconic "1-Up Mushroom" in video games, particularly the Super Mario Bros. franchise. In gaming, a "1-Up" signifies gaining an extra life, a concept that originated in pinball machines in the 1960s before being popularized by video games in the 1980s. The green mushroom in Super Mario Bros. became a widely recognized symbol for an extra life, and the term "1-Up" is now ingrained in gamer culture.

The cocktail's name is a playful homage to this video game item, evoking the idea of an "extra life" or a gaming-themed drink. This particular recipe is a modern tiki creation, as described by the "How to Drink" YouTube channel.
