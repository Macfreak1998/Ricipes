# History
The "Golden Cadillac" cocktail was created in 1952 by bartender Frank Klein (sometimes referred to as Frank Cline) at Poor Red's, a bar in El Dorado, California.

The cocktail's origin story involves a newly engaged couple who arrived at Poor Red's in their new gold-colored Cadillac, on their way to Lake Tahoe to celebrate. They asked the bartender to create a special drink in their honor. Inspired by the couple's shiny new car and the drink's slight golden tint, Klein named the cocktail the "Golden Cadillac."
