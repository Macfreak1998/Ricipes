# Zutaten
- 1/2 [[Lime]]
- 3/4 [[Lime Juice]]
- 1/4 [[Cherimoya]]
- 1 1/2 [[White Rum]]

# Zubereitung
Prepare in [[Blender]] with crushed ice
Pour into small highball glass
Add squeezed lime half

# Served
* Glass: [[Highball Glass]]
* Ice: [[Crushed Ice]]
* Garnish: squeezed [[Lime]]
