## Beschreibung

Applejack is America's oldest native spirit and a fascinating category in its own right. Originally, "Applejack" referred to an apple brandy made through freeze concentration - colonists left fermented apple cider outside in winter, the water froze, and the high-proof alcohol remained liquid. Today, Applejack is mostly a blended brandy.

The most famous representative is Laird's Applejack, a blend of 35% Apple Brandy and 65% neutral grain spirit. The result is an accessible, fruity spirit with distinct apple notes, but less complexity than pure Apple Brandy. Laird's also produces a 100% Apple Brandy (Bonded or Straight), which is more intensive, more complex, and more expensive - similar to a good Calvados.

**Taste profile:** Fresh and cooked apples dominate, accompanied by vanilla, caramel, and a warm spice. The texture is usually somewhat lighter than French Calvados, with a clear, fruity character. The alcohol content is typically 40% ABV for standard Applejack, 50% ABV for Bonded versions.

**Production:** Modern Applejack is distilled from apple cider and aged in oak barrels. The barrel aging gives it color and additional complexity with vanilla and spice notes. The blended versions are milder and more affordable, while Straight Apple Brandy is a premium product.

## Verwendung in Cocktails

Applejack bringt eine wunderbare Fruchtigkeit in Cocktails, ohne zu süß zu sein. Er funktioniert hervorragend in classicn Drinks, the ursprünglich für Brandy or Whiskey konzipiert were.

**Klassische Cocktails:**
- **[[Jack Rose]]** - Der ikonische Applejack-Cocktail with Grenadine and Lemon Juice
- **[[Applejack Rabbit]]** - Mit Orange Juice, Lemon Juice and Ahornsirup
- **[[Applejack Old Fashioned]]** - Statt Whiskey für eine fruchtigere Variante
- **[[Diamondback]]** - Mit Rye Whiskey, Chartreuse and Applejack
- **[[Jersey Cocktail]]** - Mit Cider, Angostura Bitters and Zucker

**Mixology-Tipps:** Applejack eignet sich perfekt für Herbst- and Winter-Cocktails. Er harmoniert exzellent with spices (Zimt, Nelke), anderen Früchten (Birne, Cranberry) and Honig. In manyn classicn Rezepten, the Calvados verlangen, funktioniert Applejack als günstigere Alternative - allerdings with etwas less complexity. Für tiefere, more complex Drinks lohnt sich the Griff zum Straight Apple Brandy.
