# Tabasco

## Beschreibung
Tabasco is a hot chili sauce made from fermented Tabasco chilies, vinegar, and salt. It was developed in 1868 by Edmund McIlhenny in Louisiana, USA, and is now the world's best-known hot sauce.

The sauce has approximately 2,500-5,000 Scoville (medium heat), a spicy-sour taste, and a characteristic thin, sprayable consistency.

Tabasco is available in various variants: Original Red, Green Jalape√±o, Chipotle, Habanero, and more. For classic cocktails, the Original Red sauce is usually used.

## Verwendung in Cocktails
Tabasco is used in savory cocktails to add heat and complexity. Dosage is in dashes - a few drops are enough!

Beliebte Cocktails:
- [[Bloody Mary]]
- [[Michelada]]
- [[Prairie Oyster]]

**Dosierung:** Start with 2-3 dashes and adjust to taste. Too much Tabasco dominates the entire drink.
